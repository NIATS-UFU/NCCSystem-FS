Instructions to create an executable installer (for QT applications)

1 - Copy your exe generated by the compilation process to another folder (e.g., deploy).
2 - https://doc.qt.io/qt-5/windows-deployment.html
3 - Test the application in a computer without QT.
4 - Add possible missing DLLs to the exe root folder.
5 - https://doc.qt.io/qtinstallerframework/ifw-creating-installers.html
